Cece Studio

see website

For this semester project we had two options, either use an API with content already stocked from Noroff or make our own and be responsible for our own content. I chose to make my own API for the first time, this way I would be able to design exactly what I wanted and be in total control of the product images too.

For all product images and headers I wanted to keep it spacious with the help of a blue sky background. When browsing the website you can clearly see there is a hint of blue on all the pages. With the white background and blue hints in the images the website appears clean and minimalistic.

For the product images I went after specific brands to use because of the way they photograph their products. So the shoes are in not very traditional positions in the product images. And they all have a soft light background in order to emphasize the vibe I was aiming for in this website project.

This project is clearly inspired by streetwear fashion, but I kept in mind not being too specific about the vibe. I added a small amount of grain texture to all images except the product images. So although the pictures are taken by several different photoghraphers, they still feel relatable to eachother.
