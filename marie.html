Marie

see website

Marie is my first semester project at Noroff. The focus was design, functions and how to work around with Wordpress headless CMS. I chose to go with an interior theme for this project. I thought of this as a great opportunity to showcase that the design of the website can emphasize the images and vice versa.

All of the interior blog posts in this project consist of clean architecture, clean lines and spacious rooms. I am personally very into scandinavian, minimalistic design when it comes to interior. Instead of pretending to be an interior designer, I created a fake persona that fits the projects and the entire vibe of the website.

Something that is very clear going through the website, I depend alot on white and light pastel colours here. This is because I want the images to take up most of the space and speak for themselves. I did not want to use strong contrasts or colours that would take the attention away from the interior images or illustrations.

This is why the colour palette does not consist of many colours, but they all play their own part in the design. I mixed between sans serif and serif where serif is used for titles and sans serif for paragraph/context. The titles are elegant and also play their part as a design element in the layout. While using sans serif for the context so it's easier to read.
